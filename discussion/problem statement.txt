Track 2: "Sanjeevani" — Multilingual Health Triage
Problem: In rural areas, patients often lack access to primary medical consultations and face language barriers when accessing digital health information.
Solution to build: A GenAI-powered, multilingual assistant that conducts initial health triaging by analyzing user-reported symptoms in local dialects, providing evidence-based, personalized guidance and recommending whether a patient needs immediate professional care.
How Gemma 4 should be used:

Interpret symptom descriptions in a regional language/dialect and map them to structured triage output.
Function calling to connect analysis to a next step (referral, nearby care lookup, structured logging).

What a clear problem statement looks like:
Names the specific language/dialect and population.
Frames the tool explicitly as triage, not diagnosis.
States what "evidence-based" means in their implementation.

Safety note: Submissions must include a visible disclaimer, an escalation path for emergency symptoms, and a rationale for their triage logic.
